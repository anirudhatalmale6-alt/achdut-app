ACHDUT - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ACHDUT ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). ACHDUT is a community networking app designed for mutual aid and professional networking.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ACHDUT, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile photo when you register or sign in with Google.
  • User-Generated Content: Posts, comments, images, and other content you create and share on the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used and actions taken.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for push notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features and functionality
  • Enable you to create posts, comments, and interact with other users
  • Send push notifications about activity relevant to you (likes, comments, follows)
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the platform

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure account management
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing posts, comments, and user profiles
  • Firebase Storage: For storing uploaded images
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications

All data is transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile information and content you post are visible to other users of the App.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for infrastructure services. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

ACHDUT is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or by other means. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the App's support feature.
